What is Start-up Visa?

Canada's Start-up Visa immigration program is aimed at entrepreneurs who are ready to come to Canada, set up an innovative business and develop it on the international market.

Applying for the Start-up Visa program demands that your business idea or startup should be boosted by a business incubator. For this you need to become a resident of one in Canada.
